THE AMERICAN DREAMERS

ABOUT THE BAND

Four young men from different corners of the world chose to come together to form the famous boyband, The American Dreamers. These four dreamers wished to leave the struggles of their past lives behind and achieve great heights in the American music industry. Fortunately, their American dream is being fulfilled for they have won two Grammy Awards in the past two years and are currently nominated for the next Grammy Awards.

Vidi Aldiano

An Indonesian aspiring singer who has already made a mark in Indonesia, but now has come to America to spread his music to others with the help of his band members.

Armaan Malik

A rising singer in India, Armaan is a composer and a writer and writes most of the band songs by himself. He is hear to make his mark in America.

Marco Mengoni

An Italian play back singer who has two albums of his own is now here with the band to make multiple albums with other thoughts and cultures. The music is always what people want and Marco knows how to please his fans.

Enrique Iglesias

Despite having a famous father, Enrique has always done everything by himself and never with anyones help. He has been making music since he was a little kid and now that he is older, he has come to America to give back to his fans in the form of a band.
