THE BEGINNING

Nabisco, a cookie company create by the merge of multiple cookie companies, produced the orignial Oreo in 1912. This original oreo was tampered with many time by producing different flavors and was not successful. However, the company had experience from cookie sales through their sales of animal crackers, but Nabisco had no idea that they started a never-ending oreo-demic.

Source: http://www.brandchannel.com/2017/02/23/oreo-space-dunk-challenge-022317/

THE SWITCH

Around 1954, the oreo had grown out of its thirty year old design and was redesigned to incorporate the Nabisco brand. At this point the cookie looks like the cookie that we all know and love. The creators of the Oreo then started to create advertisements for the Oreo. By 1974 the Oreo ahd gone through many titles and it was finally settled to have the name of the Chocolate Sandwich Cookie. Another major switch was when in 1990 the Oreo became healthier by replacing trans-fat with the healthier alternative of vegetable oil.

TODAY

The Oreo has definitely come a long way. Today the Oreo has multiple different flavors and products that are just as enjoyable as the Oreo's from 1912!
