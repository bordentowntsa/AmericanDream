 UPCOMING TOUR DETAILS                       Posted: March 9, 2019

After a 6 month hiatus from touring the band is back on the road stronger than ever. This year it will only consist of only American cities, but next year the band will be touring European, Asian, African, and South American countries. Each tour event, both in the US and outside of the US, will have more safety measures, but the American Dreamers wants all of their fans to be content with their experience. For this reason, the band will provide each attendee with a free $25 gift of their choice from the American Dreamers store. Along with the increased safety measures each event will have an increased number of performers. Each event will feature an aspiring dancer, singer, comedian, magician, or any other performer who wants to achieve the American Dream. The American Dreamers are excited to present their tour to the world and hope to incite the American Dream in the hearts of their fans.

 NEW ALBUMS RELEASED                         Posted: January 2, 2019

The American Dreamers have finally released two new albums "Da World" and "TSA". "Da World" is meant to represent the struggles that hinder capable people from reaching the best of their abilities. The album is meant to provide a sense of hope and encouragement for those who feel that they will never be able to reach their abilities. The songs from this uplifting and encouraging album feature rising artists from impoverished countries. Alongside the release of "Da World," the boyband has released the album "TSA" to highlight the achievements reached by Technology Student Association(TSA). TSA encourages students to utilize technology to solve the problem of the world and become strong leaders. The American Dreamers want to encourage other students to use technology and create a stronger and brighter future for the world. "TSA" and "Da World" are albums are meant to help fans of The American Dreamers bring change to the world and create a beautiful future for the world.
